PerturbOutcome Prediction Benchmark Group

We define a task for predicting responses in gene expression of single cells to chemical and genetic perturbations, aiming to measure model generalization across cell lines and perturbation types. Understanding cellular responses to genetic perturbation is central to numerous biomedical applications, from identifying genetic interactions involved in cancer to developing methods for regenerative medicine.
Furthermore, counterfactual prediction of drug-based perturbations at single-cell resolution enables cell-type specific drugs and treatments, facilitating precision medicine.
